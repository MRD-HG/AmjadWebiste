## Page Sections Breakdown

### 1. index.html - Home Page
- Navigation bar with logo and menu items
- Hero section with association name and slogan
- Quick links to main services
- Featured achievements and statistics
- Recent news/activities preview
- Photo gallery section
- Footer with contact info

### 2. about.html - About Association
- Association definition and mission
- Vision and values
- Strategic goals
- Organizational structure
- Timeline/history
- Team members

### 3. services.html - E-Services
- Digital membership form
- Volunteer registration
- Zakat Al-Seha service
- Donation forms
- Online applications

### 4. programs.html - Services & Programs
- Warmth and Feeding Campaign
- Iftar Al-Khair program
- Amjad Academy
- Medrasti Al-Hilwa
- Tabeeb Al-Ghalaba
- African Cup initiative

### 5. news.html - News
- Latest news articles
- Event announcements
- Photo galleries
- Press releases
- Success stories

### 6. contact.html - Contact
- Contact form
- Location map
- Contact information
- Social media links
- Office hours

## Design Elements
- Color palette: Soft green, gold, white
- Typography: Cairo/Noto Arabic for Arabic, Poppins for English
- RTL support for Arabic
- Responsive design for all devices
- Smooth animations and transitions
- Photo galleries and video sections
- Interactive forms
